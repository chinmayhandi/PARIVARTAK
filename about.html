About Us

Building a Sustainable Future with Clean Energy

Building a Sustainable Future with Safe Electrical Solutions

Parivartaca Power Solutions Private Limited is a Vijayapura-based company focused on delivering reliable solar energy and electrical engineering solutions.

The company operates with two major divisions: Solar Solutions Division and Electricals Division.

Electricals Division is a specialized electrical contracting and engineering firm with a “Class 1” license issued by the Electrical Inspectorate, Government of Karnataka. Based in Vijayapura, Karnataka, it provides safe, reliable and professional electrical services.

Our team includes qualified electrical engineers, supervisors, electricians, skilled wiremen and solar technicians. The team works with a safety-first approach to deliver reliable solutions and customer satisfaction.
